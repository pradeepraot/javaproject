If I switch, will T-Mobile pay my Early Termination Fees?

Individuals and families (up to 10 lines) who are currently under a postpaid contract at carriers including AT&T, Sprint, or Verizon and want to switch to T-Mobile can take advantage of the Early Termination Fee (ETF) reimbursement offer. Follow the steps below: Transfer your number(s), trade in your phone(s) and tablet(s) and receive a bill credit based on the market value for your eligible device.Then, when you get your final bill listing your carrier’s Early Termination Fee (ETF) amounts, you simply send it to T-Mobile or submit it online at switch2tmobile.com, and if you qualify, within 8 weeks you will receive a Visa® Prepaid card covering your ETF amount up to $350 per line. The card is not redeemable for cash and expires in 12 months unless extended by Issuer.
